ABOUT

Vietnamese food has been well-known and is gaining more recognition worldwide. However, Vietnamese cuisine's authenticity is fading due to the fact that not many people know how to prepare food properly and that certain dishes have fascinating facts may need some time to discover.

Pho-Real: Viet Nam on the Plate is an entertaining single-player 2D cooking game with the ambition of promoting authentic Vietnamese cuisine recipes to the players. Come with Pho-Real, the player will be immersed in Vietnamese foodie adventure, learning the recipes and gaining family value when experiencing when cooking with family members.
